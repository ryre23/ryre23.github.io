Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bisquick
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • A squeeze of lime juice
  • 1 tsp of baking powder

Instructions

  • Mix everything together - do not overmix
  • Heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan
  • Pour batter into pancakes
  • Cook until bubbly, flip
