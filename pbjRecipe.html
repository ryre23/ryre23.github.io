Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup of jelly
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  • Heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan
  • Put bread in pan
  • When bread is golden, flip
  • When golden on both sides, remove from pan
  • Spread peanut butter on bread
  • Spread jelly on bread
  • Eat
